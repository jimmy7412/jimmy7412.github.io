In Star Wars Han Solo says he travelled a distance in PARSECs, ignoring that he made it sound like a unit of time. What was that distance? A PARSEC is measured from Earth as \(\frac{1.5X10^{11}meters}{tan(\frac{1}{3600})}\) The distance in the numerator is an AU or the distance from the Earth to the sun. The whole Star Wars story take place in a galaxy far far away, so how would they know what an AU or a PARSEC is?
